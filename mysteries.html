Ireland's Pyramids I found this accidentally on google maps, so we pulled in to see what it was and found an actual pyramid in someone’s field. I googled it and found a youtube video of a guy who discovered some other pyramids. The plot thickens as there seems to be 15 pyramids around Ireland from a similar time period. Pyramid fever seemed to grip the western world, possibly with activities of the British Empire in Egypt at the time. Many of the pyramids appear to be follies, or tombs built by the landed gentry. I like to think there was a secret society, but who knows.
