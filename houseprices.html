Galway House Prices

Back to Homepage

I recently conducted a study of house prices in Galway using data from the Property Price Register. I used a mix of tools during this analysis and a full step through is available on this page. The first part includes a timelapse map of Galway showing each property sale as a coloured point on the map. Note that locations are approximate and all lat/longs were jittered from the nearest townland.

The next part includes two boxplots that you can filter accordingly. Note that it may take a minute to load as it is hosted on an external service. It currently only shows up as far as November 2025 but I hope to update it periodically.
